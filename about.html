About Angus

Angus Baker is an artist, producer and engineer from Essex, studying in London.

He started as a producer aged 16 and has progressed in the industry into a successful engineer. Now aged 23, Angus would love to share his own sound to the world.

Angus relies on his own production, engineering and mixing skills to make his music, but also loves to collaborate with peers frequently.
